To get visual arrows that show what systems schedule what jobs in the Profiler...
1. Open the Profiler (Ctrl + 7) or Window > Analysis > Profiler
2. Ensure Profiler is set to the Timeline view (visual representation of script profile)
3. Using the Additional Options Menu (...) at the top right of the Timeline section of the Profiler, select "Show Flow Events"
4. Arrows now appear on systems that schedule jobs and jobs that are scheduled by systems
5. Clicking on a system or job with an arrow, will show the full flow indicating which system schedules which jobs
